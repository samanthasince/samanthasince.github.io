Customer Review

Kim L, Denver

"I've heard great things about Cafe Faba and I'm so glad that I spontaneously went for brunch on Sunday. They have a fixed menu but always have dozens of specials running since they are seasonal farm to fresh. I highly recommend their vegan version of Eggs Benedict!"

Larry L, Aurora

"I frequent to Cafe Faba since they are around the corner from my office. Their specials are great but my favorite entree is their Avocado Toast Sandwich. Their brew is always deliciously fresh and perks me up in the morning and in the middle of the work day."

Carly A, Highlands Ranch

"Cafe Faba is a really cute place for a meal or coffee during the day and/or in the evening. The service was pretty fast despite the large crowd that was already there. I ordered a whole grain waffle with berries and a cappuccino--both which came out within 10 minutes of placing my order."

Jessica G, Lakewood

"For years I drove by Cafe Faba and would see the line out the door. I was once at the park across the street, and their brewed aroma lured me over. I was pleasantly surprised to find a loose tea and lunch joint. Their coffee perks me up in the morning and keeps me strong all day."

Larry L, Aurora

"I love that Cafe Faba is friendly to both Vegans and Vegetarians. My girlfriend and I have a brunch destination that has delicious options for both of us. If you're looking for a lowkey brunch stop that has coffee from out of this world, this is the place for you."

Carly A, Highlands Ranch

"I recently moved here from Los Angeles and had high expectations of brewed coffee and cafes. Cafe Faba does not disappoint! They have brunch specials on the weekend that my roommate and I end up sharing. Everything is so delicious, we can never make up our minds."
